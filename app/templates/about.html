  1. Add a wish
    • To add a wish, click on this icon
  2. Update rating
    • To update a wish's rating, simply click on the stars and select the new rating. Higher rated wishes are displayed at the top of the list
  3. Delete a wish
    • To delete a wish, click on the checkbox next to the wish and confirm your action. The list will be updated on confirm
  4. Marking items as bought and viewing them
    • Marking an item as bought removes it from the main wishlist view
    • Click on 'view bought items' to load items marked as bought. Click on the link again to load current/main wishlist
  5. Catogories
    • When you add a wish you select the appropriate category of the item
    • After your homepage laods, click on the dropdown beside "select filter category", choose a category and click update view
    • You will be presented with items in your wishlist from the selected category only
    • Bear in mind that you must click "view bought items" to see items marked as bought as they will not be in the main view
  6. Miscellaneous
    • Hover over a thumbnail to view further information about that wish, such as title,desc,rating and reveal the delete option. You may have to scroll down.
    • Click on a thumbnail to visit the original page that the item was viewed on. You may be able to purchase the item there.
    • Click on "share wishlist" to email wishlist URL to friends/family. Social media buttons share to respective websites.
    • You cannot add the same item/URL twice
    • You cannot visit the registration page while logged in
    • All confirmatory messages can be dismissed by clicking on the x on the far right side of the messages
    • If you click on an image or link and it says content unavailable, jus wait a second and retry
    • Click on your profile image to view your full-sized photo
    • Click on your name at anytime to return to your home screen
